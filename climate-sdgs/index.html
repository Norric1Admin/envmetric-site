    SDG-related Metrics

    Sep 28, 2020 • Environmental Statistics team

    The Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) have several indicators relevant to climate change. This article details the connections between our metrics and the SDGs, using data taken from the UK SDGs platform.

    Published

    Metrics with a connection to the SDGs, which are currently live on the site.

    • Metric 2.1.3: Concentrations of other (non-climate-forcing) pollutants is based on SDG 11.6.2.
    • Metric 3.2.1: Raw material consumption is based on SDGs 8.4.1 / 12.2.2.
    • Metric 3.4.2: Energy intensity is based on SDG 7.3.1.
    • Metric 3.4.3: Energy and pollution consumption is based on SDGs 7.1.2 / 7.2.1 / 9.1.2.
    • Metric 3.4.4: Footprints is based on SDGs 8.4.1 / 12.2.1.
    • Metric 4.1.2: Heat waves is based on SDGs 1.5.1 / 11.5.1 / 13.1.1.
    • Metric 4.1.3: Storms and gales is based on SDGs 1.5.1 / 11.5.1 / 13.1.1.
    • Metric 4.1.4: Low temperatures and heavy snow is based on SDGs 1.5.1 / 11.5.1 / 13.1.1.
    • Metric 4.1.5: Flooding or Coastal erosion is based on SDGs 1.5.1 / 11.5.1 / 13.1.1.
    • Metric 4.2.4: Clean air indicators is based on SDG 11.6.2.
    • Metric 4.3.1: Water ecosystem condition is based on SDG 6.3.2.
    • Metric 5.1.2: Circular economy is based on SDG 12.5.1
    • Metric 5.3.1: Number of countries with sustainable consumption and production (SCP) national action plans or SCP mainstreamed as a priority or a target into national policies is based on SDGs 12.1.1 / 12.7.1.
    • Metric 5.3.2: Extent to which (i) global citizenship education and (ii) education for sustainable development (including climate change education) are mainstreamed… is based on SDGs 4.7.1 / 12.8.1.
    • Metric 5.3.3: Number of countries that have communicated the strengthening of institutional, systemic and individual capacity-building to implement adaptation, mitigation and technology transfer, and development actions is based on SDG 13.2.1.
    • Metric 5.4.1: Terrestrial protected area is based on SDGs 15.1.1 / 15.2.1 / 15.4.1 / 15.4.2 (no relevant data available for 15.4.1).
    • Metric 5.4.2: Marine protected area is based on SDGs 14.5.1 / 14.c.1.
    • Metric 5.4.3: Freshwater protected area is based on SDG 6.3.2.
    • Metric 5.5.3: Investments in energy efficiency as a proportion of GDP and the amount of foreign direct investment in financial transfer for infrastructure and technology to sustainable development services is based on SDG 7.b.1.
    • Metric 6.1.1: Water security is based on SDGs 6.4.1 / 6.4.2 / 6.5.2 (no relevant data available for 6.4.2 / 6.5.2).
    • Metric 6.6.1: Disaster planning and strategies is based on SDGs 1.5.3 / 1.5.4 / 11.b.2.

    Unpublished

    Metrics with a connection to the SDGs, which have not been published yet due to data limitations.

    • Metric 3.3.1: Land cover and degraded land area is based on SDGs 6.6.1 / 11.3.1.
    • Metric 4.1.1: Drought is based on SDGs 1.5.1 / 11.5.1 / 13.11.
    • Metric 5.3.7: Number of companies publishing sustainability reports is based on SDG 12.6.1.
    • Metric 5.5.4: Amount of fossil-fuel subsidies per unit of GDP (production and consumption) and as a proportion of total national expenditure on fossil fuels is based on SDG 12.c.1.
    • Metric 6.3.8: Water demand in the built environment is based on SDGs 6.4.1 / 6.4.2.
